Peffer Photography wanted to relaunch their website with a focus on their photo prints. Inspired by the work itself, I took the brand back to its roots in the beauty of mundane Americana and cityscapes.

Overview

Client
Alex Peffer
Duration
April to June 2025
Role
UX Designer

Alex Peffer Photography is a small business with expereince working in non-profit sectors and commercial environments. What started as a passion project, Alex Peffer's business has now grown and he is looking to expand his online presence to showcase his work more effectively.

Can you tell me more about your creative process? How do people connect with your work?? What inspires you???

Solution

Focusing on the clients needs, I developed the brand into a polished identity that can reflect his vision and a new website that can invite more professional opportunities.

Problem

Alex Peffer Photography lacks a strong online presence to showcase his work, sell prints, and attract new clients.

Research Questions

  • How do people find photographers?
  • How do people hire photographers?
  • What inspires people to purchase photography prints?
  • What is the main challenge photographers face when trying to attract new clients?
