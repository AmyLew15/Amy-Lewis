Problem

In my neighborhood of Brooklyn, I noticed an increase in campaigning for the 2025 Mayoral election, so I started to wonder what the campaign process looks like in other communities.

How do people connect on local issues? Why is election coverage limited for local races? How can we stay politically active in unprecedented times?

Solution

I discovered communication and visibility issues surrounding local elections and representatives.

I created an app, Primary, to uplift local elections so voting feels like an unhurried choice rather than an overwhelming chore.
