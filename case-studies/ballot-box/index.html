Amy Lewis

Ballot Box

Close

A local election app that helps communities understand the voting process with clarity and confidence.

School

Parsons School of Design

Role

UX Designer

Duration

January to May 2025 (5 months)

  • UX/UI Design
  • UX Research
  • UX Writing
  • Prototyping
  • Wireframing
  • Illustration

Problem

In my neighborhood of Brooklyn, I noticed an increase in signage and campaigning for the 2025 Mayoral election, so I started to wonder what that looks like in other communities. How do people connect on local issues? Why is election coverage limited for local races? How can we stay politically active in unprecedented times?

Solution

I discovered communication issues surrounding local elections and the disconnect between those communities and their representatives. I created an app - Ballot Box- so voting is easy choice rather than an overwhelming chore.
